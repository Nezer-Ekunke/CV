Nezer Ekunke

My Name is Ebenezer Ukeh Ekunke, I am a 21 year old passion driven youth from Cross River state, Nigeria With major interests in technology, education, social impact and community development e.t.c.
I am a human capacity developer and love volunteering, especially in activities and projects that are of humanitarian and community services.

I possess high quality skills in project management, Microsoft appreciation, content creation and public speaking.
In my spare time, my hobbies are singing, playing musical instruments, writing, volunteering and also working as a health practitioner.
I am still open, and still looking out for more opportunities to serve my community and the nation, and also to build up myself, Learn, unLearn, reLearn and take my social impact drive to the next level.
